About Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland is a fantastical tale written by Lewis Carrol. It follows the story of a young girl, Alice, who falls down a rabbit hole into Wonderland, and tells of her adventures and heroics in the strange new place, Wonderland.

Carrol was inspired to write Alice in Wonderland from one of the children whom he told stories to, Alice Liddell, who asked for a story with “lots of nonsense.”
